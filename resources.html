Faculty Job Applications

A Guide for Applying to Tenure-Track Assistant Professor Positions

Lead authors: Tina Liu and Makoto Kelp

Contributors: Minghao Qiu, Dien Wu, and Yun Hang

[Link]

Applying to tenure-track Assistant Professor positions can be daunting and stressful! Based on our experiences from 2023-2024, we wrote a comprehensive guide to help you navigate this opaque process, from putting together an application package to preparing for interviews to negotiating your offer. Included in this guide is what we wished we knew when we applied for faculty jobs. Whether you're a postdoc or graduate student, we hope this guide will make your journey in academia a little easier.
